Featured Projects

Two projects that show how I think and work.

projectTrack

Working with AI in development is fast — but without structure, context gets lost between sessions and delivery becomes hard to track. projectTrack is a macOS app that solves this: a lightweight tool for managing AI-assisted projects, capturing decisions, tracking progress, and keeping sessions connected.

Built because I needed it. Shows how I think about workflow design, product discipline, and using AI as a delivery accelerator rather than a shortcut.

macOS AI Workflows Product Design

InvoiceReady

Invoices submitted with errors cost businesses time and money. InvoiceReady is a pre-validation platform that checks invoices against layered business and compliance rules — including NZ Peppol e-invoicing standards — before they're sent. Built full-stack with Next.js, Supabase, PostgreSQL, and TypeScript.

A genuinely complex rule engine, real compliance requirements, and product thinking baked in from the start. The kind of system where getting it right matters.
